About Us

TOP Notch Transit, LLC was established on June 12, 2020 by owner Rodney Bradshaw.

Working 12 years as a delivery driver for Sysco Foods, he understood the meaning of “Customer Service“. In December of 2021, he joined forces with Orlando Smith to reach an even greater demographic and help others realize the dream of becoming owner/operators.

They knew that if he could work with the core attitude of “Putting customers first” in starting a business, they’d have a chance at success.
